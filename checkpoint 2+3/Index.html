WHY IN TUNISIA



TUNIS..A PEACUFUL CITY! The Crime Index 2017 Mid-Year that ranks cities according to their index of crime and safety has just delivered its verdict. Of the 334 cities in the world, the Tunisian capital stands out as being one of the most peaceful with a fairly low crime index (36.11) for a relatively high security index. Even though, this ranking is accruing while terrorist invasions are at their highest level (France, Turkey, USA ...) Tunisian law and police coordination are major key-points in maintaining security (Bearing of weapon is prohibited, absence of organized crime, holdups, car jacking ... )
Tunisia has always been avant-garde in its societal choices. Choices that gave her a model status in the Arab world. Tunisians are warm in nature, like to welcome foreigners in their homeland, does not hesitate to open their doors and proudly present their culinary heritage.
